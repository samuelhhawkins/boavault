About BoaVault

Founder: Samuel Hawkins

Here at BoaVault we believe that your personal information should be secure at all times! In early 2020 Sam watched a ted talk about cyber security pushing its audience to use a different random password for each new site signed up for. This is extremely challenging to acctually put into practice and thus BoaVault was born! A simple free and easy to use password manager for everyone.
